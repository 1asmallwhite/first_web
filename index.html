Hextris

Experience the perfect blend of Tetris and hexagonal puzzle gameplay in this modern classic.

About Hextris

Hextris is a modern take on the classic Tetris game, featuring hexagonal blocks and innovative gameplay mechanics. Created by a team of passionate developers, this game combines the familiar falling-block concept with a fresh hexagonal twist that challenges your spatial awareness in new ways.

How to Play

  • Rotate the falling blocks to match the hexagonal grid
  • Clear lines by completing rows of hexagons
  • Chain combos for higher scores
  • Use special power-ups to help clear the board

Game Features

  • Responsive design that works on all devices
  • Multiple game modes and difficulty levels
  • Global leaderboard system
  • Customizable controls and themes

Open Source

Hextris is an open-source project, available on GitHub. Feel free to contribute, report issues, or fork the project to create your own version.
